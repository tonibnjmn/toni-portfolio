ZERINE

Description

Epilepsy is a neurological disorder characterized by recurring seizures caused by irregular brain activity. These seizures can be unpredictable and vary in severity, making diagnosis and management challenging. Due to the infrequent and unpredictable nature of seizures, doctors often rely on reports from those with epilepsy, their caregivers, and witnesses to gather information about seizure types, triggers, frequency, and their impact on daily life.

To address these challenges, the study proposes using wearable technology, specifically smartwatches, as a solution. These devices can monitor various metrics such as heart rate and movement, and enable communication with healthcare professionals and family members. The researchers have developed a real-time seizure detection and response system using smartwatch features like heart rate monitoring and GPS. This system aims to detect the onset of seizures and send location data to designated contacts via SMS, allowing for quick responses that could prevent injuries and complications. Additionally, it aims to create a detailed record of seizure occurrences, which could help healthcare professionals improve epilepsy management.

Scope and Limitation

The system focuses on a mobile application linked to a smartwatch that alerts users and their relatives during a seizure. The smartwatch employs heart rate monitoring, fall detection, and jerk detection to identify seizure events. It uses light pulses for heart rate measurement and an accelerometer to detect falls or convulsions. Data is transmitted wirelessly via Bluetooth to an Android mobile app, where users can monitor vital signs, view seizure history, and track episodes.

The system, however, does not diagnose or categorize seizures or provide medical prescriptions. It is designed for personal alerting and data tracking rather than clinical diagnosis. Users are advised to consult healthcare professionals for medical evaluation and care. Additionally, the smartwatch battery lasts up to 40 hours, and the device is not intended for hospital use but for preliminary study and personal monitoring.

Features

The system feature allows users to record detailed seizure information, including time, date, and duration, providing healthcare professionals with valuable data for diagnosis, thus enhancing seizure management and diagnosis accuracy. Users can input personalized BPM trigger values for warnings and alerts, ensuring accurate notifications for potential seizure activity based on individual heart rate variations. The Emergency Contact Input enables immediate communication with personnel or loved ones during an attack by storing their names and contact numbers for quick access. An Epilepsy Facts Slideshow offers insights into managing seizures and understanding the brain disorder through visual aids. Additionally, users can generate a comprehensive PDF document of all recorded seizure data for easy sharing and examination by healthcare providers.

CS107L - DROPS OF HOPE

Description

Drops of Hope is an information management system designed for Blood Donation partnerships, collaborating with medical clinics, hospitals, and other healthcare organizations to streamline and enhance blood donation drives. It offers key benefits such as significantly reducing the time spent managing data, providing robust tools for data analysis, promoting a disciplined approach to data management, transforming disparate information into valuable resources, and improving the quality and consistency of information. Drops of Hope ensures a more efficient, effective, and safer blood donation process for all involved.

Graphical User Interface

Features

Drops of Hope functions consist of several crucial modules that ensure efficient management of blood donation and blood bank operations. These include the Donation Site Information module, where users can add new site details such as location, manager, office days, and hours, as well as modify existing site information. In the Blood Extraction Site Management Module, users can add new blood bank affiliations, including their location, manager, office days, hours, telephone number, blood type, and quantity, and update or remove affiliations as needed. The Blood Bank Affiliation Management Module is responsible for managing blood stocks by adding new stock information, releasing blood type quantities to specific locations and recipients, and reserving blood type quantities. For the Blood Bank Inventory Management Module, tasks include adding and releasing blood type quantities, managing their locations, and tracking recipients. Blood Extraction Appointment details involve creating, updating, and canceling appointments.

I specifically worked on the Account Management Module, where I created and updated account information, activated or deactivated accounts, and managed password changes, gaining experience with basic CRUD operations and authentication.

PROJECTS

IT101-1L - 4 Pics 1 Word

Description

4 pics 1 word is a simple game developed using Python, where the user guesses a word based on four provided photos. The game aims to identify the common word that ties all four images together. Users can input their guesses and receive immediate feedback on whether their guess is correct. This game provides an engaging way to test and improve vocabulary and association skills through visual clues.

Graphical User Interface

Features

The "4 Pics 1 Word" game includes several essential functions to enhance gameplay. The display_photos function shows four images on the screen that provide clues to a common word. The input_guess function allows users to type in their word guesses, while the check_guess function verifies if the guessed word is correct. For additional support, the take_hint function reveals a letter from the word in exchange for coins, which are managed by the update_coins function. The pass_word function enables users to skip difficult words and move to the next set of photos. The earn_coins function rewards users with coins for correct guesses. Additionally, the track_progress function keeps a record of the user's performance, by the level of game they're playing. All these functions work together to create a seamless and enjoyable user experience.

Program Description

Asset Nest is a comprehensive centralized asset management system designed to effectively track and log all assets used within a company. This system aims to streamline asset management processes, enhance accountability, and optimize resource allocation. Its key benefits include enhanced efficiency by automating asset tracking and management, cost savings through the identification and elimination of unnecessary expenditures, improved accuracy by reducing human error, and better decision-making through detailed asset reporting.

Scope and Limitation

The scope of Asset Nest encompasses comprehensive asset management, including tracking, logging, and managing all company assets such as equipment and mobile phones. It automates asset tracking, ensures secure access for authorized users, and generates detailed asset tags and reports. The system also includes functionalities for inserting, editing, viewing, printing, and hiding asset data, as well as managing mobile phone allocations and transactions. However, its limitations include reliance on accurate initial data entry, potential integration challenges with existing systems, the need for ongoing user training and support to maximize its benefits and maintain data integrity, and lack of compliance with relevant regulations and standards. Additionally, while the system is designed to handle a growing number of assets, extreme scalability requirements might necessitate further customization or infrastructure upgrades.

Features

The system features a secure login system, allowing only authorized company accounts to access it. Users can insert asset data into a list, which is then stored in a database. For each asset, the system automatically generates an asset tag comprising the selected company, location, category, and an incremental ID. Additionally, Asset Nest offers robust data handling capabilities, enabling users to edit, view, print, and hide assets as needed.

In addition, the system includes a module for mobile phone management, allowing the company to log and manage who handles each phone used within the organization. This module tracks whether each unit is issued or sold, listing details such as the recipient, date, and reason for the transaction. This added functionality ensures comprehensive management of all company assets, providing a powerful tool for maintaining control over resources.
